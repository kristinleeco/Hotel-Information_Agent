# User Stories

Short, real-world scenarios showing the value the agent delivers to actual guests. Keep these simple and non-technical — the focus is on the *person*, not the code.

## Story 1: The Late-Night Arrival

Sara lands at 11:40 PM after a delayed flight and wants to know if she can still get food. Instead of calling the front desk and waiting on hold, she asks the hotel chatbot. In seconds, she learns the restaurant is closed but 24-hour room service offers a late-night menu, and the lobby market is open until 1 AM. She orders before she's even out of the rideshare.

## Story 2: Traveling with a Dog

Marcus is booking a weekend trip with his golden retriever and needs to know the pet policy *before* committing. The agent confirms the hotel welcomes dogs up to 50 lbs, explains the cleaning fee, and even suggests two pet-friendly restaurants within walking distance. Marcus books on the spot — a conversion the hotel might have lost if he'd had to email and wait for a reply.

> ✏️ *Customize these with the actual capabilities of YOUR agent — only claim what it can really do.*
